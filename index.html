CV · Resumé

My name is Elijah J. Mears, and I am a proud lifelong resident of North Carolina. I was born January 3, 1995, so I share a birthday with both J.R.R. Tolkien and Marcus Tullius Cicero. I grew up in Cary but consider Greensboro my home since moving here for school in 2013. I am a member of the Young Democrats of Guilford County, Director of Communications for the UNCG Democrats, and a former field organizer for a local city council campaign. Labor rights, LGBT rights, climate change, and public transportation access are all issues which I feel very passionate about and consider myself an advocate for.

I am currently wrapping up my 5th and final year as a Classical Languages & Literature major at UNC Greensboro, with an eye toward graduate school after taking a gap year. My hobbies include writing, photography, web design, and conlanging. I have had poetry published in The Coraddi, UNCG’s literary magazine, and I recently had an article published in a peer-reviewed undergraduate journal. I currently work as the Web Content Manager for The Carolinian, UNCG’s student newspaper, and I also do freelance graphics work on the side.
