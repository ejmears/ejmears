I am Elijah. I am a writer, a scholar, an amateur photographer, and a lifelong resident of North Carolina. I’ve designed and run the websites for both the UNCG Classical Society and the UNCG Democrats, as well as this one. I currently serve as the LGBT Caucus Chair for the Young Democrats of North Carolina.

Beyond academics, my hobbies include writing, music composition, web design, and photography. I have had poetry published in The Coraddi, UNCG’s literary magazine, and I am currently working on several short stories and a novel. My lifelong dream is to help make the world a bit brighter, kinder, and more equitable for everyone.

Fifth year undergraduate at UNCG majoring in Classical Languages & Literature.
