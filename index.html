CV · Resumé

My name is Elijah J. Mears, and I am a proud lifelong resident of North Carolina. I was born January 3, 1995, so I share a birthday with both J.R.R. Tolkien and Marcus Tullius Cicero. I grew up in Cary but consider Greensboro my home since moving here for school in 2013. I am the treasurer of the Young Democrats of Guilford County, LGBT+ Caucus Chair for the Young Democrats of North Carolina, First Vice Chair of the 13th Congressional District Democratic Party, and a former field organizer for a local city council campaign. My pet issues are public transportation, infrastructure, LGBT rights, and environmental conservation.

I graduated in 2018 with a Bachelor of Arts in Classical Studies from UNC Greensboro. My hobbies include writing, photography, web design, and conlanging. I have had poetry published in The Coraddi, UNCG’s literary magazine, and I recently had an article published in a peer-reviewed undergraduate journal. Currently, I do freelance graphics and web design work as I take a gap period before pursuing my graduate degree. I am also working on a fantasy novel and a short story in my free time, alongside constantly organizing and re-organizing my apartment.
