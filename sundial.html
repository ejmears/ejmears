SUNDIAL

Welcome to Sundial, a tool which takes your geographic location and uses it to calculate the times for the Roman twelve-hour day. The ancient Romans reckoned each day as being divided into twelve equal hours from sunrise to sunset, and so their hours did not line up with our own.
